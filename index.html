About

Hey, I'm Whitney! I'm located in Nashville, TN and am currently attending the Vanderbilt University Coding Boot Camp to become a full-stack web developer. I specialize in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and Bootstrap. Creating code, for me, is much like my photography. It is a means of expression into who I am and how I portray the world around me. My desire to constantly learn and to wander places far and wide has led me down the path of web development. My dream job is to be able to work remotely so that I can explore all that mother nature has to offer on this beautiful Earth.
